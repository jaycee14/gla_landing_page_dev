What is gift list assist?

  • Its a service that lets you share wish lists with relevant people - like your family or friends.
  • They can see the kind of presents you want and vice versa.
  • You can then select the gifts you intend to give but only the other group members will see that, not the recipient.

Why use gift list assist?

  • Make giving and receiving gifts easier.
  • Give friends and family gifts they really want.
  • Let others know what you'd like.
  • Reduce unwanted presents.
  • Get gift recommendations.
  • Keep track of costs.
  • Receive customised reminder emails.
  • No more messy email chains.
